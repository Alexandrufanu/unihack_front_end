How does the site help you?

The "HS Notes" site stores all the necessary information for high school subjects, separated by classes, by specialization or by chapters, reaching the desired lesson, where you will find more materials needed to understand what you are looking for. If you have a well-structured lesson you can post-it, then people can vote for your lesson and have the opportunity to comment on it.

How do you know which is the most suitable material?
They will be arranged in descending order from the most upvotes to the one with the fewest
